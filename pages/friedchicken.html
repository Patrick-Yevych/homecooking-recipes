Fried Chicken

Description

Homemade fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chicken Drumsticks with Skin
  • 1/2 cup or 8 Tbsp Butter/Margarine
  • 1.5 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Prepare mixed egg in a bowl.
  2. Mix flour with spices in another bowl.
  3. Bathe chicken in mixed egg and then in flour/spices.
  4. Fry or bake chicken.

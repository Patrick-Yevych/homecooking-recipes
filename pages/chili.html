Chili

Description

Homemade spicy chili.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/4 cups Green Bell Pepper
  • 1/4 cups Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 2 stalks Celery
  • 1-14.5 oz can Diced Tomatoes
  • 1-6 oz Tomato Paste
  • 1 cup Beef Broth
  • 1 Jalapeno Pepper
  • 1-15 oz can Chili Beans
  • 1-15 oz can Kidney Beans
  • 1 Tbsp Mexican Chili Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Oregano
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 2 Tsps Black Pepper
  • 2 1/2 Tsps Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in frying pan until it is brown. Complete this in parallel with steps 2-3.
  2. Chop green bell pepper, red bell pepper, garlic, jalapeno peppers, onion and celery.
  3. Saute chopped veggies with olive oil for 5 minutes in another pan.
  4. Add cooked ground beef, diced tomatos, tomato paste, and beef broth to the pan.
  5. Mix well and set to high heat.
  6. Add garlic, chili powder, black pepper, salt, oregano, and cumin to the pan and mix well.
  7. Add the kidney and chili beans.
  8. Cover the pan. Cook the chili on low heat for 1-2 hours stirring occasionally.
